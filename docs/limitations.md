# Limitations

This analysis is exploratory and should not be treated as trading advice.

Known limitations:

- The dataset covers a fixed historical period.
- Sentiment is measured daily, while trades may happen at different intraday conditions.
- PnL patterns can be affected by outlier trades.
- Simpler grouped comparisons may miss deeper account-level behavior.
- Past performance under a sentiment regime does not guarantee future performance.

The findings are useful for understanding behavior, but they should be interpreted with care.
