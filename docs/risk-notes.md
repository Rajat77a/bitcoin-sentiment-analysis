# Risk Notes

Crypto trading data can be heavily affected by outliers. A small number of large winning or losing trades may distort averages, especially when grouped by token or sentiment regime.

Important risk checks:

- Review both average and total PnL.
- Check whether losses are concentrated in a few accounts or tokens.
- Compare behavior across sentiment regimes instead of assuming one strategy always works.
- Treat token-specific anomalies separately from general market behavior.

These checks make the analysis more careful and less dependent on surface-level patterns.
