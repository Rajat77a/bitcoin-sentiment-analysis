# Sentiment Regimes

The Fear and Greed Index is used to group market conditions into sentiment regimes. These regimes make it easier to compare trader performance under different emotional market states.

Typical interpretation:

- Fear: cautious or risk-off market behavior
- Neutral: balanced market behavior
- Greed: aggressive or risk-on market behavior

The notebook uses these groups to compare PnL, long/short outcomes, and risk patterns across changing market sentiment.
